Detroit Shock Fan Segmentation

Multi-page Power BI analytics for revenue, ticketing, and digital engagement insights

This project analyzes fan behavior using SQL-modeled data and Power BI dashboards to support segmentation, ticket revenue analysis, merchandising insights, and digital engagement strategy for a professional sports organization.
