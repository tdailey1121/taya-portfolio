Customer Experience KPI Dashboard

Tools Used: Power BI, DAX
Built an interactive dashboard to analyze customer support metrics, helping leadership monitor team performance and customer satisfaction trends.

  • Defined business questions like average resolution & response times, % resolved, and ratings by channel & agent
  • Created custom DAX measures for % Resolved, Avg Response & Resolution Time, and Avg Customer Rating
  • Designed visuals: KPI cards, bar charts by agent/channel, donut charts by ticket volume, and slicers for Chatbot, channel, and agent analysis
  • Applied a tailored Power BI theme for clean, consistent branding

Key insights:
Tickets handled by Chat resolved 20% faster on average
Agent A had the highest customer satisfaction rating at 4.8 / 5
Email requests took longest to resolve, revealing opportunities for self-service improvements
